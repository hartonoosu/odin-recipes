Deviled Eggs Recipe

Description

These easy deviled eggs are made with hard-boiled eggs, mayonnaise, mustard, pickle juice, a touch of sugar, a pinch of salt, and pepper, and sprinkled with paprika.

Ingredients

  • 6 large Eggs
  • 1/4 cup Mayonnaise
  • 1 1/2 tsp Mustard
  • 1 tsp Pickle Juice
  • 1/2 tsp Sugar
  • 1/4 tsp Salt and Pepper
  • Pinch of Paprika

Steps

  1. Boil the eggs for 10 minutes.
  2. Transfer the eggs to ice water for 5 minutes.
  3. Half cut the eggs, remove the yolks and place them in a bowl. Mash the yolks.
  4. Stir in mayonnaise, mustard, pickle juice, salt, sugar, and pepper. Stir until creamy.
  5. Pipe the yolks by using a star tip and pastry bag into the egg white.
  6. Sprinkle with paprika and top with crispy bacon if desired.
  7. Chill until ready to serve.
